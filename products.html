Durability

GFRC has been laboratory tested and it will outlast and precast conrete, cast stone and natural stone. GFRC products are heat and scratch resistant and can withstand high moisture, weather erosion, freeze-thaw weathering, alkaline and rust compared to traditional and steel enforced conrete. It is virtually waterproof. When exposed to a flame, the conrete mixture functions as a thermal regulator. It protects the materials behind it from the flame or heat.

Lightweight

GFRC is a lightweight alternative to cast stone or precast concrete given the absence of steel and graded rock aggregates. The product is a very durable but lightweight so transportation costs are reduced significantly. It can be cast thinner, in 3/4" - 1 1/2 " thickness versus the standard 2" thickness. Now with precast materials, all GFRC products can be made to match the identical finish of traditional smooth sandstone/cast stone, granite and quartz finishes. GFRC is stronger than standard conrete but weighs up to 75% less!

Adaptability

Either sprayed or poured into a mold, GFRC can adapt to any complex and detailed shape possible, from rocks to fine ornamental detail. The product can be etched, polished, sandblasted, or stenciled. It can provide the look and feel of a natural stone, architectural precast concrete, granite or terracotta. With a smooth mix and no steel reinforcements any custom deisgn is possible and beautifully crafted.
